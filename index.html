What do I do?

Web Development

I've been developing web-based solutions for almost three years, mainly using .NET core, Blazor and MongoDB to build APIs and full-stack solutions professionally. For my pet projects, I like to use .NET core, Golang and MongoDB to develop backend solutions, while using the Vue-Pinia-Vuetify combo to create engaging UI/UX. To automate deployments and activities, I mainly use bash scripting and Python.

Cloud Development

Despite being an AWS-certified Solution Architect, I spend most of my time developing solutions in the Azure cloud. I'm well-rounded in cloud development, design, and deployments with both providers, automating my activities as much as possible using bash scripting and Terraform.

Carbs King

I have a great love for cooking, particularly when making fresh pasta and pizza. I enjoy experimenting with different techniques and methods for creating unique and flavorful pasta dishes, from coloured pasta to stuffed varieties. There's something satisfying about crafting a delicious meal from scratch, and I take great pride in what I create.
